# Runic Races

**Choose what you *are*.** Runic Races is a flagship [Origins](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/origins-forge) add-on that turns the origin screen into a two-step heritage choice: pick a **family**, then a **race** within it. **37 races across 7 families**, each hand-built on one clean promise — **one signature ability, one passive strength, and one real weakness.** No race is strictly best; every one changes *how* you play, not just *how well*.

## The Seven Families

- **Human** *(gold)* — adaptable generalists: Primian, Celeron, Magi, Valen
- **Elven** *(magenta)* — arcane grace, frail bodies: High Elf, Dark Elf, Moon Elf, Blood Elf, Ice Elf
- **Dwarven** *(slate)* — tough and subterranean: Deep One, Forge One, Frost One, Iron One, Sky One, Runic One
- **Bestial** *(green)* — sharp senses and predatory instinct: Arachnid, Avian, Canine, Feline, Kitsune, Serpen
- **Faeborne** *(teal)* — magic, illusion, and gossamer wings: Changeling, Dryad, Sprite, Nymph, Faerie
- **Undead** *(purple)* — undeath's gifts and the sun's curse: Zombie, Skeleton, Wraith, Demon, Reaper
- **Draconic** *(red)* — elemental breath, scaled hide, and flight: Fire Drake, Ice Drake, Sea Serpen, Terra Drake, Volt Drake, Wind Wyrm

## Signature Moments

Theatrical, never spammy. A Feline cheats death with **Nine Lives**. A Reaper rises at its own grave. The six drakes each **breathe their element** — fire, frost, drowning tide, shattering earth, the thunderbolt, and a gale that hurls foes skyward. Faeries weave a **glamour** that blesses allies and curses enemies in one breath. Every ability carries its own sound, particles, screen cue, and family-tinted HUD.

## Strengths *and* Weaknesses

Balance is zero-sum. The Sprite is a +30%-speed glass cannon. The Wind Wyrm is the freest flyer alive — and a wretched, weakened thing the moment it's caged underground. Drakes are immune to their element and burned by its opposite. The Undead shrug off poison and hunger but **decay in sunlight**. Counterplay always exists: gear, helmets, the right terrain.

## Look the Part *(with Pehkui)*

Every race has its own **height**, clustered by family so a party reads at a glance — tiny darting fae (Sprite & Faerie at less than half height), short and stocky dwarves, tall lithe elves, looming demons and reapers, and towering drakes that fill a doorway. From 0.45 to 1.30, you don't just *play* a race — you *look* like one.

## Deep Mod Integration *(all optional)*

- **Ars Nouveau / Iron's Spellbooks** — per-race mana, spell cost, and spell damage
- **Curios** — family accessory slots (Elven necklace, Dwarven belt, Faeborne ring, Undead charm)
- **Apotheosis** — luck modifiers • **Pehkui** — per-race scaling • **Feathers** — stamina-gated mobility

Absent mods are silently skipped — Runic Races runs great standalone.
